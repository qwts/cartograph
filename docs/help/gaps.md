# Gaps & Drift register

The honest slice recovery could not confirm, in three lanes that never
conflate: **System gaps**, **Unsupported patterns**, and **No-evidence
questions**; drift findings have their own tab.

- At scale, gaps group into **cause classes** (stop reason × extractor),
  ranked with flow-blocking classes first. Expand a class for paged
  instances.
- **Escalate class locally** runs every instance as one durable job — one
  staged proposal per instance, accepted or rejected individually. Nothing
  joins the graph unaccepted. Cloud escalation stays per-instance because a
  consent grant binds to one exact payload.
